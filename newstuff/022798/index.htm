..."An Alan Smithee Film: Burn Hollywood Burn" is an insider's hoot with layers of badass sass --- the hysterical film producer played by Ryan O'neal, and Richard Jeni impresses as the compromising studio head dealing with the megabucks moviemaking madness in this bimbo sendup. Along with Sly Stallone, the cast includes Eric Idle as looney Alan Smithee, Whoopi Goldberg, Jackie Chan, Gavin Palone, Sandra Bernhard, Coolio, Chuck D, Leslie Stephanson (a curious look-alike for Elizabeth Berkley, who starred in Joe's cult favorite, "Showgirls"), Harvey Weinstein (in a role as a private investigator that could lead to more offers), Billy Bob Thornton, Bob Evans, Bob Shapiro, Billy Barty, Larry King, among others...
