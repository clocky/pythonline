In 1662, Sir Thomas Pygge (a gentleman fop) and Jaymey Lamprey (a pretty boy) were walking in the Mall "discussing of ye affairs of state, and admiring of ye pretty servant girles and the ladies with their pretty boobies" casting glances now and then at "the military men in their attractive breeches" when the aforesaid Jayme Lamprey was suddently struck about the head and neck with an object which did fall from the sky causing him such a terrible injury that he did immediately expire of the sudden unexpected blow.
