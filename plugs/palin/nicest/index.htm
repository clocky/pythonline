News from Michael Palin
HE HAS BEEN FOUND ALIVE!
Notes from the star trailer by Michael Palin

No sooner had I stepped off the plane (unfortunately a split second before it reached the jetty) than I was stopped by a group of men disguised as John Cleese (the masks were uncannily lifelike but as three of them were under five foot tall it was a bit of a give-away). Anyway one got on the other's shoulders and offered me a part in a new film, written by John Cleese, and called "Fierce Creatures". I told them I had already appeared in a film called "Fierce Creatures" and that the experience had affected me so badly I had been forced to hide in the Pacific Rim for a year. The man told me that they were doing some extra shots on the film and that I would have a very good time and my dressing room would always be full of strawberries and if I ever wanted to learn French, there were girls available. I said thank you very much but I had a book to write about my journey around the Pacific Rim and it would take me until the new year to finish it. He then punched me hard in the face and said that Mr. Cleese was not used to being turned down and that if I didn't co-operate he could get nasty and start using some Dutch words. So I agreed to be in the film "Fierce Creatures" for the second time, and when I arrived at the studio and the let me out of the trunk of the car who should I see but my old friends Jamie Lee Klein and Kevin Curtis I was especially pleased to remember their names as it was a year since I last saw them. "Hi Ronnie" they cried. It was as if no time had passed at all.

My dressing room is small but has a bed, chair, bucket and a bell which I can ring for assistance. On set I am required to dress as a bee. John says that everything I say is much funnier if I am dressed as a bee. (He himself was dressed as a kangaroo for a while which was convenient as he could keep his script and copy of "Swedish Bombshells" in his pouch). He is not keen to let anyone look at his copy of "Swedish Bombshells". He says it's all about transport. Well cycling, mainly. He showed me some of the pictures of bicycles I asked him why they all had stark naked women next to them. He called me a dickhead and said it was to stop them falling over.

It's hot being in a bee suit, even in England in September. It's made of concentric layers of sponge rubber and coated with alternate stripes of yellow and black fur (artificial fur of course, DON'T WORRY ABOUT THAT! Highly inflammable but politically correct, it says on the label).

But now it's all over. Late last Saturday - Sept 9th, I was woken in the middle of a terrible dream that Bob Dole was measuring my inside leg and a face peered in through the bars of my dressing room and told me that it was all right for me to go home. But as I tried to slip out of my bee costume for the last time, I realised I was not alone…..
