A soggy Palin waves cheerfully as he is pulled out of the lock at Teddington after the silliest Python bit ever - the frankly stupid Fish Slapping Dance, which we shot as part of a 16 minute Python insert in a Eurovision programme celebrating May Day. We shamelessly recycled the piece in the next Python series as it was so gloriously inane, and is still the favorite sketch of many of us.
